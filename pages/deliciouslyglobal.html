Deliciously Global

A modern and responsive site for small business

This site was created for one of my local clients, Deliciously Global. The business was without a website and approached me about creating something for them that satisfied their need to connect with potential clients, but also would fit within their marketing budget and would be completed by their specified deadline.

Using a combination of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Sass, and Git, this site performs well on all screen sizes, uses metadata for search engine optimization, and follows accessibility standards to ensure that clients searching for their services will be easily able to find them, contact them through a custom form, and book appointments.
